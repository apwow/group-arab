About Arabian Group

Arabian Group is a dynamic and forwardthinking holding company established in 2023. With a commitment to driving innovation, fostering growth, and creating value across diverse sectors in the Iraqi Market, Arabian Group stands as a beacon of excellence in the business landscape.

Our Mission

At Arabian Group, our mission is to lead and inspire. We strive to empower businesses within our portfolio to achieve their full potential while contributing positively to the communities we serve. Through strategic investments, collaborative partnerships, and a dedication to excellence, we aim to shape a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Vision

To be the foremost catalyst of innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth, enriching lives and driving positive change across diverse industries and communities.
This vision statement encapsulates Arabian Group's aspirations to lead by example, fostering innovation, forging strategic partnerships, and making a meaningful impact on society and the business landscape as a whole.

Our Story

• Once upon a time, in the bustling city of Baghdad, a group of ambitious entrepreneurs came together with a shared dream: to build something remarkable. Fueled by a passion for innovation and a desire to make a difference, they founded Arabian Group in 2023.
• Today, Arabian Group stands as a symbol of innovation and opportunity, a beacon of hope in a world of endless possibilities. As it continues to grow and evolve, Arabian Group remains committed to its founding principles, always striving to make a positive impact on the world around it.
